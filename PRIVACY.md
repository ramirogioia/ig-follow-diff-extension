# G-Follow Inspector — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-12

## What data we access
- Your Instagram followers and following lists, and the derived differences (who you follow that doesn’t follow you, and vice versa).
- Your Instagram username (to identify your own profile in the lists).

## How data is processed
- All processing happens locally in your browser while you run the scan.
- Results are stored locally in `chrome.storage` so you can view them in the popup.
- No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

## What we do not collect
- No passwords, session cookies, tokens, or other authentication secrets.
- No browsing history outside of `instagram.com`.
- No analytics or tracking.

## Permissions
- `https://www.instagram.com/*` host permission to read the followers/following UI you see in your own session.
- Chrome extension permissions: `storage`, `tabs`, `windows` (for the worker window layout).

## Contact
If you have questions about this policy or the extension, contact the publisher as listed in the Chrome Web Store.
